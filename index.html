Grilling Guide: Delicious Steak

Follow these steps to grill the perfect steak:

  1. Choose a quality steak cut, such as ribeye or sirloin.
  2. Preheat your grill to high heat (around 450-500Â°F).
  3. Season the steak with salt, pepper, and your preferred seasoning.
  4. Place the steak on the grill and sear each side for 2-3 minutes for a medium-rare doneness.
  5. For well-done, cook for an additional 2-3 minutes per side.
  6. Let the steak rest for a few minutes before serving to retain juices.
  7. Enjoy your perfectly grilled steak!
